Justrite Safety Group: Multi-Brand Ecommerce Redesign

Unifying 6 industrial safety brands under one cohesive B2B ecommerce ecosystem

Project Overview

A comprehensive UX redesign of the Justrite Safety Group's multi-brand ecommerce platform, featuring 6 distinct safety equipment brands: Justrite, Eagle, Checkers, NoTrax, and specialized divisions for hazardous storage, spill control, and workplace safety.

Company

Justrite Safety Group - Global leader in industrial safety solutions

Scope

6 brand websites + unified mega-menu system

Role

Lead UX/UI Designer

Timeline

18 months (2024-2025)

The Challenge

Justrite Safety Group had acquired multiple safety equipment brands over the years, each with its own website and navigation structure, creating a fragmented and confusing customer experience.

Fragmented Experience

Customers struggled to navigate between brands and find products across the portfolio.

Inconsistent Design

Each brand had different navigation, terminology, and user flows.

Poor Product Discovery

Complex product categories made it difficult for B2B buyers to find the right safety solutions.

Cross-Brand Shopping

Customers often needed products from multiple brands but couldn't shop across brands easily.

The 6 Brands

🏭 Hazardous Storage

Safety cabinets, flammable storage, containment solutions

💧 Spill Control

Spill kits, containment pallets, absorbent materials

⚠️ Motion Safety (Checkers)

Traffic safety, cable protectors, warning systems

🦺 Workplace Ergonomics (NoTrax)

Anti-fatigue mats, entrance systems, safety flooring

🏗️ Industries

Industry-specific safety solutions and compliance

📚 Safety Resources

Training, compliance guides, safety best practices

Research & Discovery

Research Activities

  • 30+ interviews with B2B customers
  • Analytics review across all 6 brands
  • Competitive analysis of industrial sites
  • Information architecture audit
  • User journey mapping

Key Insights

  • 65% of customers bought from 2+ brands
  • Navigation was cited as #1 pain point
  • Buyers wanted industry-specific views
  • Mobile usage was growing rapidly

Solution: Unified Mega-Menu System

We designed a revolutionary mega-menu system that provides brand-specific navigation while maintaining a consistent structure across all 6 brands.

4-Column Layout

Consistent mega-menu structure: Featured Products | Shop by Category | By Industry | Resources

Brand-Specific Content

Each brand's menu is customized with relevant products, categories, and resources.

Visual Hierarchy

Hero images, icons, and clear typography guide users to the right products.

Cross-Brand Navigation

Easy access to other brands and unified shopping cart.

Design System

Created a comprehensive design system that balances brand individuality with system-wide consistency.

Shared Components

  • Mega-menu structure (4-column grid)
  • Product cards and filters
  • Search interface
  • Shopping cart and checkout
  • Typography and spacing system

Brand Customization

  • Brand colors and logos
  • Hero imagery and icons
  • Product category organization
  • Industry-specific content
  • Brand voice and messaging

Key Features Designed

🎯 Smart Product Discovery

Advanced filtering by compliance, industry, material, size, and more.

🏢 Industry-Specific Views

Tailored product recommendations for manufacturing, chemical, oil & gas, etc.

🛒 Unified Shopping Cart

Shop across all 6 brands with a single cart and checkout.

📱 Mobile-First Design

Responsive mega-menu that adapts perfectly to mobile devices.

✓ Compliance Center

OSHA, NFPA, and EPA compliance guides integrated into product pages.

📚 Resource Library

Training materials, spec sheets, installation guides, and best practices.

Key Learnings

  • Consistency doesn't mean identical - each brand needs room for its identity
  • B2B buyers value efficiency over aesthetics, but both are important
  • Industry-specific content significantly improves conversion
  • Mobile optimization is critical even for B2B industrial buyers
  • Compliance information must be easily accessible throughout the journey
