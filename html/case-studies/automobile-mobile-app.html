Discount Tire Mobile App

America's most trusted tire retailer, now in your pocket

Project Overview

A comprehensive iOS mobile application for Discount Tire that enables customers to browse tires, schedule appointments, track installations, and manage their vehicle garage on the go.

Company

Discount Tire - America's #1 tire retailer with 1,100+ stores

Role

Lead UX/UI Designer

Platform

iOS (iPhone & iPad)

Timeline

8 months (2024)

The Challenge

Discount Tire needed a mobile app to meet modern customer expectations for convenience and digital-first experiences.

Customer Pain Points

  • Difficult to find the right tires for their vehicle
  • Scheduling appointments was phone-only
  • No way to track installation status
  • Managing multiple vehicles was cumbersome

Business Goals

  • Increase mobile appointment bookings
  • Reduce call center volume
  • Improve customer retention
  • Compete with digital-first competitors

User Research

We conducted extensive research to understand customer needs, behaviors, and expectations.

Research Activities

  • 25 user interviews
  • Survey of 500+ customers
  • 5 in-store observations
  • Competitive analysis of 12 apps
  • Analytics review of website behavior

Key Insights

  • 78% wanted mobile appointment scheduling
  • Users struggled to identify tire sizes
  • Price transparency was critical
  • Installation tracking reduced anxiety

User Personas

Jessica - Busy Professional

Age: 35 | Tech Savvy: High

Goals: Quick tire purchases, minimal phone calls, convenient scheduling

Frustrations: Limited time, hates phone calls, values convenience

Tom - DIY Enthusiast

Age: 52 | Tech Savvy: Medium

Goals: Research tire specifications, compare options, best price

Frustrations: Overwhelming options, unclear specifications, price confusion

Key Features

🚗 My Garage

Add and manage multiple vehicles with automatic tire size detection and purchase history.

🔍 Tire Browse & Compare

Advanced filtering, side-by-side comparison, and detailed specifications for informed decisions.

📅 Appointment Scheduling

Book installations with real-time availability, store selection, and confirmation.

📍 Store Locator

Find nearby stores with directions, hours, services, and real-time availability.

🔔 Installation Tracking

Real-time status updates from check-in to completion with push notifications.

⭐ Rewards Integration

View and redeem loyalty rewards, track points, and get exclusive offers.

Design Process

1. Onboarding Flow

Designed a welcoming 3-step onboarding to introduce key features and encourage account creation.

2. Vehicle Setup

Streamlined process to add vehicles using VIN scanning or manual entry with intelligent defaults.

3. Tire Selection

Created an intuitive browse experience with filters, sorting, and comparison tools.

4. Appointment Booking

Simplified multi-step flow: select store → choose date → pick time → confirm.

Usability Testing

Testing Methodology

  • 3 rounds with 18 participants
  • Task-based scenarios
  • Remote and in-person sessions
  • Prototype testing with Figma

Key Improvements

  • Added tire comparison feature
  • Simplified appointment flow
  • Enhanced vehicle setup
  • Improved navigation structure
