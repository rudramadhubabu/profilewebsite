AI-Powered School ERP System

Transforming Indian school management with AI-driven insights and unified dashboards

Project Overview

A comprehensive AI-powered ERP system designed specifically for Indian schools, featuring role-based dashboards for super admins, school admins, teachers, parents, and students.

Scope

Multi-tenant SaaS platform for 100+ schools

Platforms

Web Dashboard + iOS/Android Mobile Apps

Timeline

12 months (2023-2024)

Role

Lead Product Designer

The Challenge

Indian schools were using fragmented, outdated systems for attendance, grading, communication, and administration, leading to inefficiency and poor parent engagement.

Manual Processes

90% of schools still used paper-based attendance and grading systems.

Poor Communication

No unified channel for teacher-parent communication, leading to missed updates.

Lack of Insights

Admins had no data-driven insights on student performance or school operations.

Compliance Issues

Difficult to maintain compliance with Indian education board requirements.

User Personas

Super Admin (Org Owner)

Manages: Multiple schools, billing, global settings

Needs: High-level analytics, school performance comparison, revenue tracking

School Principal/Admin

Manages: Single school operations

Needs: Student records, teacher management, attendance overview, academic calendar

Teacher

Uses: Mobile app primarily

Needs: Quick attendance marking, grade entry, parent communication, class schedule

Parent

Uses: Mobile app

Needs: Child's attendance, grades, notifications, teacher chat, fee payment

Key Features

🎯 AI-Powered Insights

Predictive analytics for at-risk students, performance trends, and personalized recommendations.

📊 Unified Dashboards

Role-specific dashboards with real-time data visualization and KPI tracking.

📱 Mobile Apps

Native iOS/Android apps for teachers and parents with offline support.

✅ Smart Attendance

Biometric integration, QR code scanning, and automated absence notifications.

💬 Communication Hub

Secure messaging, announcements, and automated parent notifications.

📚 Academic Management

Grade books, report cards, curriculum planning, and exam scheduling.
