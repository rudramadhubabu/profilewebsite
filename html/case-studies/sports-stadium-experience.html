iOS Mobile Feature

Sports Stadium Social Experience

Connecting fans in-stadium with live chat, reactions, and social features

Project Overview

An innovative iOS app feature that transforms the in-stadium experience by enabling fans to connect with friends, chat in real-time, share reactions, and make match predictions together.

Role

Lead UX/UI Designer

Platform

iOS Mobile App Feature

Timeline

4 months (2024)

Team

2 Designers, 5 Engineers, 1 PM

The Problem

Fans attending matches felt disconnected from their friends seated in different sections and lacked engaging ways to interact during the game.

Pain Point 1

Friends sitting in different sections couldn't easily communicate or share the live experience together.

Pain Point 2

No way to express reactions or emotions to exciting game moments with fellow fans.

Pain Point 3

Limited social engagement beyond passive spectating reduced overall stadium experience value.

Pain Point 4

Difficulty finding and connecting with friends at the stadium before and after the match.

User Research

Research Methods

  • 20 in-stadium fan interviews
  • Survey of 300+ season ticket holders
  • 3 contextual observation sessions
  • Competitive analysis of 10 sports apps
  • Social media behavior analysis

Key Insights

  • 85% wanted to chat with friends during games
  • 72% would engage with live reactions
  • Fans missed key moments while texting
  • Predictions/betting heightened engagement

Key Features Designed

💬 Live Stadium Chat

Real-time group chat with friends at the stadium, synced with game events.

🎭 Quick Reactions

Tap to send instant emoji reactions to goals, saves, and exciting moments.

🎯 Match Predictions

Predict scores and outcomes, compete with friends on leaderboards.

👥 Friend Finder

See which friends are at the stadium and easily coordinate meetups.

🎉 Goal Celebrations

Immersive full-screen celebrations when your team scores.

📊 Live Stats Integration

Access real-time game statistics without leaving the social view.

Design Process

1. Stadium Entry Experience

Designed seamless check-in flow that automatically activates social features upon stadium entry.

2. Immersive Match View

Created an AR-like view showing game stats overlay without blocking the live action.

3. Chat & Reactions

Designed quick-access chat panel with one-tap reactions for instant engagement.

4. Social Integration

Built friend invitation system and prediction leaderboards to drive virality.
