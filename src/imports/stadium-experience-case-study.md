Act as a senior product designer creating a professional UX case study for a new mobile feature called:

Sports Stadium Experience

The feature allows fans watching a live sports match on their mobile device to enter an immersive stadium experience.

Inside the experience, users can watch the match with friends, chat, react, cheer, make friendly predictions, and celebrate together.

The case study should look like a real product design project presented in a professional portfolio.

Maintain a balanced layout with approximately 50% text and 50% visual content such as images, diagrams, and UI examples.

SECTION 2 — PRODUCT OVERVIEW

Explain the concept.

Millions of fans watch sports games alone through streaming platforms.

While convenient, it removes the social energy of watching a game in a stadium with friends.

The Stadium Experience feature recreates the social environment of live sports watching inside a mobile app.

---

SECTION 3 — PROBLEM STATEMENT

Describe the challenge.

Fans watching sports remotely often experience:

Lack of social interaction
No shared excitement with friends
Limited engagement during matches
No easy way to celebrate key moments together

Fan interviews
Sports streaming behavior analysis
Observation of social media interactions during games

Key insights:

Fans constantly message friends during matches
Fans react emotionally to goals and highlights
Fans enjoy making predictions during games
