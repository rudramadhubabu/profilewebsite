DESIGN SYSTEM OVERVIEW

The Stadium Experience interface follows a cinematic sports viewing design language.

The UI is designed to immerse fans inside a digital stadium environment.

Key design characteristics include:

Dark stadium video backgrounds
Glass-style translucent overlay panels
Orange gradient primary actions
Circular fan avatars
Floating reaction controls
Minimal HUD-style interface elements.

SCREEN 2 — IMMERSIVE STADIUM VIEW

Once inside the stadium, the interface rotates into landscape orientation.

The match video fills the screen to create an immersive stadium viewing environment.

Minimal HUD elements appear:

Match score and timer at the top
Fan count indicator.

---

SCREEN 3 — FRIEND PRESENCE PANEL

A vertical panel on the left side displays friends currently inside the stadium.

Each friend is represented by a circular avatar.

Users can:

View active friends
Mute disruptive participants
Kick users if needed.

PRODUCT IMPACT

The Stadium Experience feature transforms traditional sports streaming into a shared digital stadium.

Benefits include:

Higher viewer engagement
Stronger social interaction
More immersive sports viewing experience
Gamified fan participation through predictions and reactions.

---

CASE STUDY CONCLUSION

The Stadium Experience creates a new category of interactive sports viewing.

By combining live streaming, social interaction, fan reactions, and prediction gameplay, the product recreates the emotional excitement of watching a match inside a stadium—anywhere in the world.
