3. UX Problem Statement

Prompt:

Write a detailed UX problem statement.

Problem

Industrial safety professionals must quickly find compliant equipment for hazardous environments.

However current platforms present major UX barriers:

Product discovery is slow due to complex navigation.

Multiple brands create fragmented experiences.

Technical compliance data is difficult to understand.

Product comparison is inefficient.

Navigation structures vary between brand websites.

Users often need to visit multiple brand websites to find solutions.

This results in:

wasted time

procurement delays

increased compliance risk

The challenge is to design a unified UX system that simplifies discovery across all brands.
