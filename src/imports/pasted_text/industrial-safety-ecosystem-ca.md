Designing the Industrial Safety Ecosystem

Subtitle

Reimagining how safety professionals discover, evaluate, and implement workplace safety solutions across multiple industrial brands.

Context

The Justrite Safety Group operates several specialized industrial safety brands including:

Justrite – chemical storage and flammable safety cabinets

Checkers – jobsite safety equipment

NoTrax – industrial floor safety matting

Eagle – hazardous material storage and containment

Each brand has its own website, product structure, and navigation patterns.

This fragmentation creates challenges for safety professionals trying to find the right solutions quickly.

2 — PROJECT CONTEXT

4 — UX PROBLEM STATEMENT

Industrial safety professionals often need to identify equipment quickly to maintain regulatory compliance and prevent accidents.

However, the current ecosystem presents several UX challenges.

Problem 1
Fragmented navigation across brands.

Users must move between multiple brand websites to find complementary safety solutions.

Problem 2
Complex product discovery.

Products are categorized differently across each site.

Problem 3
Information overload.

Technical product specifications are difficult to scan.

Problem 4
Compliance complexity.

Users must verify regulatory compliance manually.

Problem 5
Long product evaluation process.

Industrial buyers must compare multiple products across brands.

8 — KEY RESEARCH INSIGHTS

Insight 1

Users think in terms of hazards, not product categories.

Example

User goal

Store flammable liquids safely

System requirement

Recommend cabinets, spill containment, safety cans.

Insight 2

Users search by problem, not brand.

Example

Need ground protection for construction vehicles.

User does not care if product is Checkers or another brand.

Insight 3

Compliance indicators are critical.

Users rely on visual compliance badges.

Examples

OSHA compliant

NFPA certified

EPA approved

Insight 4

Technical documentation must be easily accessible.

Industrial buyers frequently download:

product spec sheets

safety certifications

installation manuals
