KEY UX INSIGHTS

Insight 1

Industrial safety purchasing is solution oriented, not brand oriented.

Insight 2

Users think in workplace hazards, not product categories.

Insight 3

Compliance guidance is critical to purchasing decisions.

Insight 4

Cross-brand product discovery is currently fragmented.

RESEARCH CONCLUSION

The analysis reveals a strong product catalog ecosystem but a fragmented user journey across brands.

Key improvement opportunities include:

Unified safety solution navigation

Cross-brand product discovery

Compliance education visibility

Guided product selection
