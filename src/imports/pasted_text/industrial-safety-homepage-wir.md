A Unified Product Discovery Platform for Justrite Safety Group
SECTION INTRODUCTION

The homepage of an industrial safety platform must perform significantly more functions than a typical marketing website.

Unlike consumer ecommerce sites, industrial buyers arrive with specific safety problems, regulatory compliance requirements, and technical constraints.

Therefore the homepage must simultaneously support:

• product discovery
• compliance education
• cross-brand navigation
• technical documentation access
• distributor connections
• enterprise purchasing workflows

The challenge is to design a homepage that provides access to thousands of industrial safety products without overwhelming the user.

CASE STUDY STORYTELLING SUMMARY

This homepage redesign transforms the Justrite Safety Group platform from a brand-centered catalog of independent websites into a unified industrial safety ecosystem.

By reorganizing navigation around safety solutions, hazards, and industries, the platform becomes significantly easier for safety professionals to navigate while still preserving the expertise of each brand.
