## 📊 HEUR ISTIC ISSUES IDENTIFIED

### Nielsen's 10 Heuristics - All Covered

1. **Visibility of System Status**
   - Users unsure of current system state
   - Led to confusion and errors

2. **Match Between System and the Real World**
   - Technical jargon difficult for non-technical users
   - Unfamiliar terms throughout interface

3. **User Control and Freedom**
   - Users felt trapped in workflows
   - No easy way to undo or navigate back

4. **Consistency and Standards**
   - Inconsistent UI patterns across sections
   - Varying terminology created confusion

5. **Error Prevention**
   - Lack of clear error messages
   - Poor validation mechanisms

6. **Recognition Rather Than Recall**
   - Complex workflows to remember
   - Increased cognitive load

7. **Flexibility and Efficiency of Use**
   - Not optimized for frequent users
   - Too many steps for common tasks

8. **Aesthetic and Minimalist Design**
   - Cluttered and visually overwhelming
   - Difficulty focusing on important information

9. **Help Users Recognize, Diagnose, and Recover from Errors**
   - Unhelpful error messages
   - No clear guidance for fixing issues

10. **Help and Documentation**
    - Reliance on external documentation
    - Reduced user self-sufficiency
